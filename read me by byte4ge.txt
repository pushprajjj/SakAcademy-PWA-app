to change full apprearance of mew app

1.change name by going res/values/string.xml
2. package name by going gradle scripts/build.gradle(module) and change applicationId
3. change project name by going gradle scripts/settings.gradle and change rootProject.name
4. change icon by right click on res folder/select image assets/ and choose images
5. splash scree change by putting logo.png in res/drawable
